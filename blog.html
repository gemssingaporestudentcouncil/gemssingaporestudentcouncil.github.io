First Student Council Meeting

20/06/2019

To begin the new president (Shaquille Aranas) and vice president (Sam Poder) were introduced by Josh (former Student Council President). They then explained the importance of student council and the commitments required by members. This year, student council will be split into four core divisions; Academics (Lead by Diego Paniagua Silva), Art (Lead by Dhafin Rizaldi), Sport (Lead by Anushka Bhatia) and Service (Lead by Anjali Grillo). Annika Nair will be joining the leadership team as the treasuer of the council.
