Hello! We're Student Council. We work as a link between the student body and the school's administration to create change for the students.

WHAT WE DO

Student Competitions

Student Council has organized muliple competitions ranging from Basketball matches to Spelling Bees. This bring together the student body and generate school spirit

Service / Fundraisers

Student Council is always proud to use their abilities towards enabling better lifes for others. For this reason we organize events were students can help charities as well as direct communities.

Put Forward Policies

As the elected representatives of the student body, the student council works to find ways to improve their school life.
